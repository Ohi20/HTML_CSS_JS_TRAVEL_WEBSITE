welcome to Sikkim

It's time to start your adventure

Sikkim is a small state located in the northeastern part of India, bordered by Bhutan, Tibet, and Nepal. This beautiful state is known for its breathtaking views of the Himalayas, vibrant culture, and delicious food.

One of the top attractions in Sikkim is the capital city of Gangtok. With its clean streets and beautiful architecture, Gangtok is a must-visit destination. You can take a stroll through the town and explore its many markets and cafes, or head up to the Tashi Viewpoint for stunning panoramic views of the Himalayas.
